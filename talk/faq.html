Password strength meta
Beware of your password security! Just don't even make a password here. Set openids / oAuth, preferably in all of them: GitHub, Google, Twitter, etc. But picking 2 is good enough to have a backup, in case one of them crashes. Also, feel free to make a strong password, by all means. Right now it's enforced for at least 21 characters.
